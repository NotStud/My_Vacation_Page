Lodging Options

Valamar Argosy Hotel

Nestled near Dubrovnik's old town, the Valamar Argosy Hotel receives accolades for its modern rooms, complete with contemporary comforts and breathtaking views, though a handful of guests find the space somewhat limited. The cleanliness is regularly celebrated, with travelers impressed by the pristine conditions.

Hotel Bellevue Dubrovnik

Strikingly set on a 30m high cliff on Miramare Bay, Hotel Bellevue Dubrovnik boasts some of the most spectacular panoramas of the Dalmatian coast. From the moment you arrive, you're immersed in a world of contemporary luxury and elegant service making you feel as if you're on your own private ocean liner.


Places to Eat Nearby

Bon Appetit Bistro - Pizzeria

Bon Appetit Bistro - Pizzeria is a charming eatery located in the heart of Zagreb, Croatia. With its cozy atmosphere and delicious cuisine, it's a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike. At Bon Appetit Bistro, you can indulge in authentic Italian flavors with a Croatian twist. From traditional wood-fired pizzas to mouthwatering pasta dishes, there's something on the menu to satisfy every craving. The bistro's warm and welcoming ambiance makes it the perfect place to enjoy a leisurely meal with family and friends. Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite or a relaxed dinner, you're sure to be impressed by the friendly service and delectable food at Bon Appetit Bistro - Pizzeria.

Restaurant Prova Dubrovnik

At Restaurant Prova, diners can expect a menu inspired by the rich culinary traditions of the Dalmatian region, featuring fresh seafood, locally sourced ingredients, and innovative twists on classic Croatian dishes. From succulent grilled fish to tender octopus salads, each dish is expertly prepared to tantalize the taste buds and showcase the flavors of the Adriatic coast.
