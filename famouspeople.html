Luka Modrić

Accomplishments: Professional footballer, FIFA World Cup winner (2018), Ballon d'Or winner (2018)

Additional Information:

  • Played for Real Madrid and Tottenham Hotspur
  • Named Croatian Footballer of the Year multiple times
  • Recipient of numerous individual awards and accolades

Nikola Tesla

Accomplishments: Inventor, electrical engineer, AC current pioneer

Additional Information:

  • Contributed significantly to the development of alternating current (AC) electrical systems
  • Invented the induction motor and the Tesla coil
  • Holder of numerous patents and inventions

Toni Kukoč

Accomplishments: Former professional basketball player, NBA champion (Chicago Bulls), Olympic silver medalist

Additional Information:

  • Played for the Chicago Bulls during their championship-winning years alongside Michael Jordan
  • Known for his versatility and basketball IQ
  • Inducted into the FIBA Hall of Fame

Mate Rimac

Accomplishments: Founder of Rimac Automobili, pioneering electric vehicle technology

Additional Information:

  • Founded Rimac Automobili at a young age, focusing on electric vehicle technology
  • Rimac Concept One, the company's first production car, gained international acclaim
  • Continues to innovate in the field of electric mobility
