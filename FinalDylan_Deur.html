Transportation in Croatia

Croatia offers various transportation options including buses, trains, and taxis. The country is well-connected by a network of highways and public transportation.

From Airport to Hotels

From the airport, you can take a taxi, shuttle service, or public transport to reach your hotel. Major airports like Zagreb, Split, and Dubrovnik offer convenient connections to city centers and popular destinations.
